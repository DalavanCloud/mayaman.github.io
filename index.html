resume

they love me, they love me not


This machine simulates the practice of plucking petals off of a flower to determine another's "true feelings". In this work, I chose to infinitely oscillate between two statements, thus representing many peoples’ train of thought as they pursue love.

skills: Processing, Firmata, Arduino

#HandsUpDontShoot


Inspired by recent episodes of police brutality, the Black Lives Matter movement, and the "Hands Up Don't Shoot" slogan adopted by protestors responding to to the shooting of Michael Brown in Ferguson. This game like interaction aims to cause contemplation of the role that guns, games, and power dynamics play in real life interactions both very recently and further in the past.

skills: Processing

Dark Matter

House of Blues


Inspired by a house down the street, House of Blues listens to the amplitude and frequencies within the song "Pick up the Pieces" by Average White Band and brings the jazz to life through color and movement.

skills: Music Visualization, p5.js, p5.js Sound Library, JavaScript
