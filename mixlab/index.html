Mixlab

Winner of the 2018 Webby Award in the "Mobile Sites and Apps: Music" category.

MixLab is an experiment that makes it easier for anyone to create music, using simple voice commands. It is available on the Google Home by saying "Hey Google, talk to MixLab," or you can also try it on the site.

I designed the voice controlled user interface/experience, developed the web prototype, and explain the experiment in the video above.

Created in collaboration with the team at the Google Creative Lab and friends. See more information on its experiment page.
